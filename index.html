Background Information

I come from Bao Loc city - a city located in the mountainous area of the middle part of Vietnam. It is a wonderful place full of gorgeous landmarks and lovable people. Another part of Bao Loc which I really like is it's weather. You will love Bao Loc's weather as it is subtle, cool and stay the same around the year. Although it has many cool things, Bao Loc is still a small city which is not very well known. Nevertheless, it will always in my heart!

My family is consists of 5 members: Me, my parents and my 2 sisters. My parents are famers and they grow coffee trees. My sisters is also students like me. One is studying to become a doctor, another is a 7 grader.

Okay now back to me, how am I decided to major in Software Engineering? Well it's has something to do with my childhood. Since I was a little boy, I have always played with my family computer and messed with it, often broken it and gotten myself into troubles. You could say that I attached to computer since my childhood and now I thing that a career related to computer would be a great choice to me!

Currently, I am studying in Software Engineering at FPT university, Ho Chi Minh city. I hope that I will graduate quickly with good score and then landing a good job.
